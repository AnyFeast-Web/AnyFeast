Nutritionist Consultation Portal Requirements
Purpose
Build a secure portal for nutritionists to capture deeper customer information during a 1 to 1 consultation that is not collected during initial onboarding.
Primary users
Nutritionists / coaches
Admin team
Future: customer view only access if needed
Core objective
Enable nutritionists to open a customer profile, review existing onboarding data, and add structured consultation notes that support personalised nutrition guidance.
Key screens
1. Login and access
Secure login for authorised nutritionists only
Role based access
Session timeout
2. Customer search and profile
Search by name, email, phone, customer ID
View customer summary
Existing onboarding data visible in read only mode
Consultation history timeline
3. Consultation form
Structured sections for the nutritionist to complete during or after the session:
A. Medical and health history
Previous diagnoses
Current symptoms
Digestive issues
Allergies and intolerances
Family history
Medication history
Previous surgeries or major illnesses
B. Lifestyle and behaviour
Sleep pattern
Stress levels
Energy levels
Physical activity
Work schedule
Smoking
Alcohol
Hydration
C. Nutrition history
Past diets followed
What worked / did not work
Meal timings
Cravings
Binge eating / emotional eating patterns
Cultural food preferences
Food aversions
D. Supplements and treatments
Current supplements
Past supplements taken
Brand, dosage, frequency, duration
Reason for taking
Side effects or outcomes
Other treatments or programmes followed
E. Blood reports and test results
Upload blood reports, scans, prescriptions, PDFs, images
Key manual entry fields for important markers
Example: HbA1c, glucose, cholesterol, vitamin D, B12, iron, thyroid, liver markers
Date of test
Comments by nutritionist
F. Goals and constraints
Weight goals
Health goals
Budget
Cooking ability
Time available
Family / household context
Religious or dietary restrictions
G. Consultation notes and plan
Free text notes
Priority issues
Agreed next steps
Follow up date
Internal notes not visible to customer
Functional requirements
Auto save draft
Save and continue later
Edit previous consultation entries
Time stamped entries with nutritionist name
File upload with preview
Structured dropdowns, checkboxes, text fields, date fields
Validation for required fields
Mobile responsive but desktop first
Clear section navigation
Searchable consultation history
Security and compliance
Encrypted data storage and transfer
Consent checkbox before storing medical information
Audit trail of who viewed or edited records
Role based permissions
GDPR aligned handling of health data
Nice to have
Flag missing critical data
Highlight abnormal blood marker values
Generate summary note after consultation
Export consultation PDF
Front end expectations
Clean dashboard style UI
Fast form loading
Accessible design
Simple step by step consultation flow
Reusable components for forms, uploads, notes, and timelines
Definition of done
Nutritionist can search customer, open profile, review onboarding data, add consultation details, upload reports, save notes, and view past history in one place.
